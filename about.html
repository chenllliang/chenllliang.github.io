Intro.

Hi, this is Liang Chen (陈亮). I'm a phd student at the Institute of Computational Linguistics, Peking University advised by Baobao Chang.

Currently, I am interested in employing multimodal Large Language Models to enhance embodied agents. I am also interested in multilingual NMT and semantic parsing. Please feel free to contact and discuss. Google Scholar Page
